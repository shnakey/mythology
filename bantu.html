Bantu Mythology

The Bantu faith is a belief and legends of native peoples in Africa. Even though Bantu peoples make up a large number of ethnically diverse groups, they are relatively homogeneous in terms of cultures and customs as with the Bantu languages. Many Bantu cultures traditionally believed in a supreme god whose name is a variation of Nyambe/Nzambe.
